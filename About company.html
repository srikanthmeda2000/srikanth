ABOUT US

Nendrasys is a specialist company in e-commerce and e-business. We are change agents who transform enterprises irrespective of the size of their business. We help enterprises set up and manage online shops, level the playing field, increase their business's exposure and reach out to their customers globally.

As a digital transformation accelerators and e-Commerce Implementation Partners, we synergise the best solutions for different processes, enterprise applications, and payment gateways to create high-performance e-commerce business models for B2B, B2C, and B2B2C. We deliver top-rated e-Commerce software and Omni-channel solutions to help enterprises across the globe innovate faster and sell more.

Kalyan Chigurupati is the Founder and CEO of Nendrasys. He is an entrepreneurial engineer, who has one foot solidly planted in the engineering profession while guiding exceptional service ideas to the marketplace.
